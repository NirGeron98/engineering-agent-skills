# Runbook Writer Checklist

- [ ] Trigger, audience, scope, impact, prerequisites, permissions, and stop conditions are defined.
- [ ] Existing runbooks, alerts, dashboards/logs, service docs, ownership, rollback docs, and incident history were inspected or requested.
- [ ] Read-only diagnosis steps come before mutation.
- [ ] Each command/query has purpose, expected healthy/unhealthy output, and safety notes.
- [ ] Mitigation, rollback, or mutation steps include approval, blast radius, and validation.
- [ ] Escalation path, communication, cleanup, and known failure modes are documented.
- [ ] Unverified commands, dashboard links, owners, or permissions are marked pending.
- [ ] The runbook can be followed by someone who was not part of the original incident.
