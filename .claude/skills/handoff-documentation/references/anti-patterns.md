# Handoff Documentation Anti-Patterns

## Completion theater

- **Symptom**: The note says work is done while tests or evidence are missing.
- **Why it matters**: The next person trusts an unsafe state.
- **Correction**: Label done, pending, blocked, and unverified work separately.

## Context dump

- **Symptom**: The handoff is a transcript or raw notes.
- **Why it matters**: The reader must reconstruct the path.
- **Correction**: Organize by status, evidence, decisions, risks, and next steps.

## Lost failures

- **Symptom**: Failed commands, rejected hypotheses, or blocked attempts disappear.
- **Why it matters**: The next person repeats dead ends.
- **Correction**: Include failed checks and what they ruled out.

## Memory-only handoff

- **Symptom**: The handoff relies on recollection instead of diff, logs, or outputs.
- **Why it matters**: Important details drift.
- **Correction**: Inspect artifacts or mark exact evidence as pending.
