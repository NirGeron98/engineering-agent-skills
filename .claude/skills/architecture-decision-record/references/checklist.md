# ADR Checklist

Use this before the final handoff.

- [ ] Located existing ADR location, numbering, status terms, and style if present.
- [ ] Stated the problem and decision context in neutral language.
- [ ] Captured constraints, non-goals, owners, and evidence links.
- [ ] Listed considered options, including rejected alternatives.
- [ ] Explained why the chosen option won under the constraints.
- [ ] Documented positive and negative consequences.
- [ ] Included migration, operational, compatibility, or maintenance impacts where relevant.
- [ ] Added a revisit trigger, not just a vague "revisit later".
- [ ] Added follow-up actions and owners or marked ownership pending.
- [ ] If decision history, ADR convention, docs, diagrams, or repo evidence were missing, emitted exact evidence requests and marked the ADR section pending.
